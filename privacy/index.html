Privacy Notice

For the AI Readiness Health Check at bigtre.business · PDPA (Thailand Personal Data Protection Act) compliant · Last updated 2026-05-26

What we collect

When you use the AI Readiness Health Check, we collect:

  • Your answers to the 10 questions (operational state of your business)
  • Your name, work email, and company name
  • Your optional free-text note ("anything we should know")
  • Basic technical metadata: browser user agent, country derived from IP, UTM parameters from the link you arrived through

Why we collect it

To deliver the personalized readiness roadmap you requested, and to enable a founder of Big Tre to follow up personally about how we might help your business.

Where it goes

Submissions are stored in a single Google Sheet accessible only to the founders of Big Tre. Emails are delivered via Resend, our transactional email provider. No data is shared with any other third party, sold, or used for marketing automation beyond the direct founder follow-up.

How long we keep it

24 months by default. You may request earlier deletion at any time (see below).

Your rights under PDPA

You may, at any time:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Withdraw your consent and request deletion of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Thailand Personal Data Protection Committee (PDPC)

To exercise any of these rights, email napat@bigtre.business. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

This page and the Health Check do not use tracking cookies. Cloudflare Turnstile (used to prevent bots on the form) may set short-lived security cookies.
